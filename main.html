Are you a cat lover?

Did you know that cats are domesticated subspecies (Felis silvestris catus) of feline animal, commonly kept as a house pets. They are usually valued by humans for companionship and for their ability to hunt vermin. Cats are often small and furry. 3 Signs That Say You're a Cat Lover: 1. Cat hair is your everyday accessory. 2.You're vigilant about the cat litter box. 3.Your cat is a card-carrying member of your family.

Do you know they are color blind?

Cats have fewer cones than humans, so they see fewer colors. A cat's vision is similar to that of a color blind person. They just may not see the “true” color of an object Cats can see shades of blue and green, but red and pink can be confusing. These might look greener, while purple might look like another blue.

Do you know what does that mean they make trill or coo sound?

Dr. Phillips says: Cats usually trill, or coo like a pigeon, when they're happy and saying hello. “Mom cats will also use this sound to get the attention of their kittens and trilling is almost always a sign of a happy cat.” It's a sweet way to say hi.
